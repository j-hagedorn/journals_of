Herschel) Observer Independent (later) discoverer RH … DS Rect. & Decl. for 2000.0 (from Revised NGC/IC) Vmag Visual magnitude (from Revised NGC/IC) Con Constellation (from Revised NGC/IC) Type Object type (from Revised NGC/IC) S Object status: 1 = galaxy, 2 = galactic nebula, 3 = planetary nebula, 4 = open cluster, 5 = globular cluster, 6 = part of nebula, 7 = NGC dublicate, 9 = star(s), 10 = not found ID1, ID2, ID3 Messier number; double identifications in NGC or IC
